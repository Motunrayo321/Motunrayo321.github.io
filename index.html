Technical Implementation

  • Fully Analog Design: No microcontroller used - pure analog circuitry for maximum reliability and minimal power consumption. Transistor-based switching and timing circuits
  • Solar Charging: 12V solar panel with charge controller circuit preventing battery overcharge/overdischarge. MOSFET-based PWM charging for optimal battery life

  • Battery Management: Lead-acid/Li-ion battery with undervoltage protection. Automatic dusk-to-dawn operation using LDR light sensor

Design Features
